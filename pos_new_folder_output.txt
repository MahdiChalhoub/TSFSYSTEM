?? Concept:
In Depot Vente, stock is transferred to the client without immediate sale. The invoice reflects only items actually sold by the client. You only charge when they declare or report a sale.

?? Lifecycle & Rules
Step
Depot Vente
Normal Sale

1??
Products shipped to client location
Products sold to client

2??
No immediate invoice or "Provisional" invoice
Invoice is created and locked immediately

3??
Client reports sold items
Sale invoice created with declared quantities

4??
Return unsold items if needed
No returns unless refund

5??
Only sold quantity billed and locked
Entire quantity billed immediately

6??
Client is billed on verified sold quantity
Full invoice generated at sale time

