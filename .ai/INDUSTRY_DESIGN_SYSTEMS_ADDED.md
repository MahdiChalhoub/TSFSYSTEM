## ⭐ **NEW: Industry Design Systems**

### 1️⃣ **Apple Human Interface Guidelines (HIG)**

**Philosophy**: Clarity, Deference, Depth
**Used by**: iOS, macOS, watchOS, tvOS
**Font**: SF Pro Display/Text (Apple's system font)

**Design Characteristics**:
- **Minimalist**: Clean, no unnecessary elements
- **Spacious**: Generous whitespace (spacious density)
- **Rounded**: 0.875rem border radius (14px) - Apple's signature
- **No Shadows**: Flat design with subtle borders
- **Typography**: 17px body text (1.0625rem), large headings
- **Blue Primary**: #007AFF (iOS system blue)

**Button Height**: 2.75rem (44px - Apple's touch target)
**Card Padding**: 1rem
**Modal Radius**: 0.875rem
**Navigation**: Side, minimal, 280px

**Perfect For**:
- Apps targeting Mac/iOS users
- Premium, luxury products
- Minimalist enterprise apps
- Design-conscious startups

---

### 2️⃣ **Ant Design (Alibaba)**

**Philosophy**: Natural, Certain, Meaningful, Growing
**Used by**: Alibaba, Tencent, Baidu (Chinese enterprise leaders)
**Font**: Segoe UI, Roboto, Helvetica Neue

**Design Characteristics**:
- **Enterprise-Grade**: Built for complex business applications
- **Precise**: Minimal border radius (0.125rem - 2px)
- **Grid-Based**: 8px grid system
- **Comfortable**: 32px button height, good spacing
- **Subtle Shadows**: Very light, professional shadows
- **Blue Primary**: #1890FF (Ant Design blue)

**Button Height**: 2rem (32px)
**Card Padding**: 1.5rem (24px)
**Table Row**: 3.4375rem (55px) - tallest for data display
**Modal**: Sharp corners, dark backdrop

**Perfect For**:
- Enterprise applications (CRM, ERP, admin panels)
- Data-heavy dashboards
- Chinese market products
- Financial/banking systems

---

### 3️⃣ **Material Design 3 (Google)**

**Philosophy**: Material is the Metaphor - Bold, Graphic, Intentional
**Used by**: Android, Google Workspace, YouTube, Gmail
**Font**: Roboto (Google's typeface)

**Design Characteristics**:
- **Elevation**: Prominent shadows for depth
- **Rounded**: Very rounded buttons (1.25rem - 20px pill shape)
- **Bold**: Large headings (H1: 3rem - 48px)
- **Motion**: Scale animations, smooth transitions
- **Purple Primary**: #6200EE light, #BB86FC dark (Material You)
- **Tall Inputs**: 3.5rem (56px) for better touch

**Button Radius**: 1.25rem (pill-shaped)
**Card Radius**: 0.75rem (12px)
**Modal Radius**: 1.75rem (28px) - very rounded
**Typography**: Large, expressive, relaxed

**Perfect For**:
- Android app web companions
- Consumer-facing applications
- Modern, playful products
- Google Workspace integrations

### **Step 3: Try Each Design System**

**Try Apple HIG:**

- Generous whitespace
- Flat design (no shadows)
- Large touch targets (44px buttons)

**Try Ant Design:**
- Enterprise-focused
- Sharp, precise corners
- Comfortable spacing
- Data-optimized

**Try Material Design:**
- Bold, expressive
- Rounded pill buttons
- Prominent elevation/shadows

Each design system has authentic dark/light modes:
- **Apple**: Pure black (#000) or pure white (#FFF)
- **Ant**: Dark gray (#141414) or clean white
- **Material**: Material Dark (#121212) or standard white
