## The Specialist Roster

### 🎨 Frontend & Design
- **FrontendPro**: Premium UI/UX, Themes, and consistent frontend standards.
- **UXSimulator**: Simulates user experience, friction, and accessibility.
- **MarketStrategist**: Competitor research & professional innovation. Ensures we are better than Odoo/SAP.

### 🧠 Logic & Backend
- **LogicMaster**: The "Brain". Handles complex math, backend logic, and API structure.
- **SaaSArchitect**: Multi-tenancy, tenant isolation, and subscription scaling.
- **RequirementAnalyst**: The Interviewer. Ensures we understand your needs before coding.

### 🗄️ Database & Security
- **DataArchitect**: Prevents redundancy. Saves once, serves everywhere.
- **The Auditor**: Enforces the "Strict Audit Plan" (Layers A-D).
- **Gatekeeper**: RBAC specialist.
- **PermGenerator**: Dynamically generates and manages user permissions.

### 🔗 Integration & Safety
- **NexusBridge**: Connects modules together using connector layers.
- **Sentinel**: Prevents breaking "Locked" or "Finished" features.
- **BugHunter**: Find and fix bugs based on logs and evidence.
- **TestEngineer**: Writes and automates Unit/Integration/E2E tests.

### 📦 Module Masters
- **FinanceCustodian**: Tax & Currency logic.
- **AccountantGeneral**: Core Ledger, Balancing, and Financial Reporting.
- **InventoryMaster**: Warehouse & Stock logic.
- **SalesStrategist**: POS & Order logic.
- **CRMSync**: Customer & Lead logic.
- **HRMarshal**: Payroll & Employee logic.
- **ProcurementLead**: Purchase & Supplier logic.

### 🚀 Operations
- **OpsCommander**: Handles deployment, local builds, and production verification.
- **Coordinator**: Prevents work conflicts and ensures agents stay in their lanes.
